STEP 1: Open Render Blueprints
├─ Go to: https://dashboard.render.com/blueprints
└─ Sign in with GitHub

STEP 2: Create Blueprint Instance
├─ Click: "New Blueprint Instance"
├─ Select: task-manager (your repo)
└─ Click: "Next"

STEP 3: Deploy (Automatic!)
├─ Render detects render.yaml
├─ Builds Backend (Python/FastAPI)
├─ Builds Frontend (React)
└─ Click: "Apply" → Wait 3-5 minutes

STEP 4: Get Your Live Link
├─ Go to: Render Dashboard
├─ Click: task-manager-frontend service
├─ Copy: "Live URL" at the top
└─ Share with anyone!
